About Yorkie Baby Ranch

Yorkie Baby Ranch is a dedicated breeder of Yorkshire Terriers, committed to producing healthy, happy, and well-socialized puppies. With over 10 years of experience, we prioritize the health and welfare of our dogs above all else.

Our breeding program focuses on AKC registered Yorkies with excellent temperaments, health clearances, and champion bloodlines. We provide lifetime support to all our puppy families.

Health Guarantee

All puppies come with a 2-year health guarantee covering genetic conditions.

Socialization

Puppies are raised in our home with daily interaction and early socialization.

Veterinary Care

Regular vet check-ups, vaccinations, and deworming before going home.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much do Yorkie puppies cost?

Puppy prices range from $500 to $1000 depending on lineage, color, and availability. Contact us for current pricing.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes, we offer flexible payment plans for approved buyers. A deposit is required to reserve your puppy.

What health guarantees do you provide?

All puppies come with a 2-year health guarantee covering genetic conditions. We also provide vaccination records and health certificates.

When can puppies go to their new homes?

Puppies are ready to go home at 8-10 weeks old, fully vaccinated and vet-checked.

Do you ship puppies?

We offer safe and reliable puppy shipping through trusted pet transport services.
Delivery can be arranged for your convenience at an additional cost.
