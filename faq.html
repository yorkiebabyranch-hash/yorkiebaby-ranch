Frequently Asked Questions

How much do Yorkie puppies cost?

Puppy prices range from $500 to $1000 depending on lineage, color, and availability. Contact us for current pricing.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes, we offer flexible payment plans for approved buyers. A deposit is required to reserve your puppy.

What health guarantees do you provide?

All puppies come with a 2-year health guarantee covering genetic conditions. We also provide vaccination records and health certificates.

When can puppies go to their new homes?

Puppies are ready to go home at 8-10 weeks old, fully vaccinated and vet-checked.

Do you ship puppies?

We offer safe and reliable puppy shipping through trusted pet transport services.
Delivery can be arranged for your convenience at an additional cost.
